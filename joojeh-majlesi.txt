recipe for joojeh-majlesi:

ingredients:
joojeh
yogurt
lemon juice
olive oil
salt and pepper
zaffran

instructions:
marinate joojeh in zaffran
marinate in olive oil for at least one hour
add lemon juice, yogurt, salt and pepper and leave at least 2 hours

grill or broil
dip in melted butter and zaffran when grilling

Enjoy!
